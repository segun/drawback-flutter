## Description

### Full Description (4000 characters max)

DrawkcaB is a unique social platform that combines real-time chat with collaborative drawing. Connect with friends, express yourself through art, and have conversations that go beyond words.

**Key Features:**

🎨 **Collaborative Drawing Canvas**
Draw together with friends in real-time on a shared canvas. Watch your creativity come to life as you and your chat partner create art together.

💬 **Real-Time Chat**
Send chat requests to other users and start conversations instantly. Stay connected with your friends through our secure messaging system.

🔒 **Privacy First**
Control your visibility with public and private modes. Choose who can find and connect with you.

✨ **Clean & Intuitive Interface**
Beautiful design that puts your conversations and creativity front and center. No clutter, just you and your friends.

🎯 **Easy to Use**
Simple registration process, intuitive controls, and a user experience designed for everyone.

**Perfect For:**
- Artists who want to collaborate in real-time
- Friends who want a more creative way to chat
- Anyone looking for a unique social experience
- Creative brainstorming sessions
- Long-distance doodling with friends

**How It Works:**
1. Create your account with a unique @displayname
2. Search for friends or discover new people
3. Send chat requests to connect
4. Start drawing and chatting together

**Privacy & Safety:**
- Secure authentication with email verification
- Full control over your profile visibility
- Easy account management and deletion options
- Encrypted connections for all communications

Join the DrawkcaB community and discover a new way to connect with friends through the power of collaborative creativity!

### Notes for Reviewer
DrawkcaB is a real-time collaborative drawing and chat application. To test the full functionality:

1. Log in with the provided demo account
2. Use the search feature to find other users
3. Send a chat request to connect with another user
4. Once accepted, you can draw together on a shared canvas
5. All drawing actions are synchronized in real-time

The app requires an internet connection to function as it relies on real-time WebSocket connections for the collaborative features.

For testing purposes, you may need two devices or you can create a second test account to see the full bi-directional functionality.
